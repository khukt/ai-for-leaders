Core Records

Operational data determines whether the workflow reflects reality

These are the records the organisation already relies on to run work. If they are fragmented or weak, AI inherits the same weakness.

What It Contributes

Workflow context, business records, entity histories, and the operational reality the system is supposed to support.

What Fails When Weak

Predictions, summaries, routing, and decisions reflect stale or fragmented records instead of live conditions.

What Leaders Usually Miss

Model access cannot compensate for operational fields that are missing, poorly defined, or disconnected from the real workflow.

What To Ask Next

Are the records behind this use case current, complete, and close enough to real work to support dependable outputs?

Key idea: most AI data failures are not about one missing dataset. They come from confusing different data roles and underinvesting in the weakest one.
