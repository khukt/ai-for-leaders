Artificial Intelligence

Broad systems for prediction, reasoning, perception, and decision support

Machine Learning
Systems that learn patterns from data
Deep Learning
Multi-layer neural networks made practical by compute, data, and better training methods
Generative AI
Models that create text, images, code, audio, or synthetic media

Often built with deep learning and foundation-model architectures.

Leadership point: these are related but not interchangeable categories. Different layers imply different data needs, costs, explainability constraints, and governance risks.
