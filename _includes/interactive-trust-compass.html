Trust Compass for live AI use

Pick a check to test one part of trustworthiness. The compass is meant to be a fast executive screen before a team treats an AI system as ready for live use.

Executive Screen Test each direction before treating live reliance as earned.
Ready For Live Use? Reliable

The system should behave consistently enough for the real situations where people are expected to rely on it.

The compass is strongest when it forces a pause: one weak direction should lower confidence in live reliance.

Leadership Question

Is the system dependable enough across the situations where we expect people to rely on it?

What To Look For

Task-specific testing, known limits, monitoring after deployment, and a clear view of when performance degrades.

Weak Signal

The team can show a demo, but cannot show how the system behaves across edge cases, drift, or operational pressure.

Management Move

Narrow the use case, raise the evidence bar, and avoid dependence until reliability is proven in the real workflow.

Key idea: if one direction is weak, the right response is usually not faster scaling. It is to strengthen safeguards, ownership, or evidence before live reliance grows.
