# Chapter Writing Tracker

This tracker reflects the current manuscript state in `_book/` and is meant to guide writing order, revision effort, and editorial focus.

## Status Key

- `Strong draft`: substantial draft exists and the core argument is already present
- `Working draft`: meaningful content exists, but the chapter still needs expansion and sharper sectioning
- `Outline stub`: the chapter has framing and intent, but not enough developed content yet
- `Write late`: best completed after the main chapters are mature
- `Placeholder`: structure exists, but the content is still to be created
