## What you’ll get from this book

- **Clarity:** what AI is (and isn’t), and what’s changing now  
- **Direction:** where AI creates value (and where it doesn’t)  
- **Control:** governance, accountability, and vendor oversight  
- **Compliance:** laws, regulations, and financial exposure  
- **Leadership:** how to lead people through AI-driven change
